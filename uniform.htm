Uniform

 

The Regina uniform consists of a skirt, white or navy blue collared shirt, knee socks, and saddle shoes.  Each incoming class is assigned a unique blue and white plaid-patterned skirt which they keep for all four years of high school.

 

 

Saddle Shoes are available through

Schoolbelles, and Verfaillie & Cossette

Shoes - located at 23515 Nine Mack Dr. in St. Clair Shores

  Polo shirts and sweatshirts are available at the RHS Bookstore
