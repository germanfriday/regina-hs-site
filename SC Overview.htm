Regina SPOrTS Club

The SPOrTS Club was founded in 1997.  SPOrTS is an acronym for Saddlelite Parents Organized To Support, and was organized for the purpose of supporting and promoting the Regina High School Athletic Department and its athletes.  The SPOrTS Club meets the first Wednesday of every month at 7:00 PM in the boardroom. All monthly meetings are open to the members at-large.  The Executive Board reserves the right to call private Board Meetings as needed.

SPOrTS Club Meeting -
The SPOrTS Club meets the first Wednesday of every month during the school year. Meetings are at 7:00 PM in the Boardroom (between the Main Lobby and Saddles Café ).
