The Purpose of the FLC:

The purpose of the Foreign Language Club is to promote multicultural awareness and world peace through presentations, field trips, and hands-on experiences.

The Colors of the FLC:

The colors of the Foreign Language Club are blue and yellow to symbolize loyalty and friendship.

The Flower of the FLC:

The flower of the Foreign Language Club is the daisy

Motto of the FLC:

The motto of the Foreign Language Club is, "In union there is strength."

Song of the FLC:

The song of the Foreign Language Club is, "It's a Small World."
