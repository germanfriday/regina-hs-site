To be an active member of the forensics team you must:

    1. Go through the category selection process (described below)
    2. Attend all required meetings (our meetings are on 1st and 3rd Wednesdays after school)
    3. Sell bagels one morning each month; sell cookie dough
    4. Help out for 1/2 hour at Open House
    5. Meet at least once per week with a coach and practice your piece
    6. Compete in at least 3 tournaments during the season.

 

Active members can earn a letter in forensics after 2 years and can earn 1/2 credit in fine arts

Optional Workshop Dates the following dates are optional workshop/information meeting dates that can benefit new Forensic team members. Current members are encouraged to attend:

September - presentation of Public Address categories and Limited Prep categories. Hear about extemp, impromptu, broadcasting, informative, sales and oratory; presentation of Interp categories. Hear about storytelling, dramatic interp, serious, prose, dramatic duo, poetry, and muliple categories.

October 5 - piece bash. Workshop for any student who still needs a piece. We will meet in room 303.

Cookie Dough Sale Required participation. Sell cookie dough over Thanksgiving break. Dough arrives in time for baking up those wonderful Christmas cookiesJ

Regina Open House Required participation Sunday, November 4 from 1-4 p.m. You are expected to represent the Forensics Team for at least 1/2 hour during that time. Let Mrs. Sawicki know when you are available. Minimum of two girls per time slot.

Preliminary Tournament Dates: To attend a particular tournament you MUST practice your piece with Mrs. Sawicki no less than three times in the 2 weeks leading up to the tournament.
