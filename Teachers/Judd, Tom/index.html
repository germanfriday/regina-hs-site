TECHNOLOGY

I am the Technology Director for Regina High School. I support, monitor, and maintain the RHS data and voice network. This includes administration of all data/voice network equipment, software, accounts, and policies as directed by the Regina High School administration.

Connected to the outside world by a T1 line, the RHS data/voice networks connect students and staff to the World Wide Web, enabling them to accomplish educational activities and processes, as well as providing communication with other Internet, websites, and telecomm users throughout the community, region, country, and world.

SPOrTS Club

In addition to working with Regina technology, I am the Moderator of the RHS SPOrTS Club. This is a group of parents and staff working for the betterment of the athletic environment at Regina High School. The Saddelite Parents Organized To Support Club was founded - by me and several other interested parents - in the summer of 1997. Since then we have provided thousands of dollars in support, hundreds of volunteers, and many worthwhile events for student athletes and sports programs at RHS.

Regina Robotics Team

The Robotics Team was organized in late December of 2009 as the “SaddleBots”. With the support and backing of school administration, a VEX Robotics Kit was purchased, along with a myriad of additional supplies and equipment. Students who wanted to participate began meeting regularly beginning the third week of January 2010.  On February 13, the SaddleBots competed in their first VEX Tournament at neighboring De LaSalle High School, placing twelfth of fourteen teams. Five weeks later, with a new design, many hours of building, testing, and rebuilding, the SaddleBots placed seventh out of sixteen teams at MSH Spartan Challenge in East  Lansing, MI.
