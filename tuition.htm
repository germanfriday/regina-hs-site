Tuition

Dear Parents,

You have an important financial decision to make for your family. You must decide how to finance your childs tuition at Regina High School . In order to help you make this decision, I offer you important information about how to afford this investment for your daughter.

What is the lifetime value my child will receive from an education at Regina High School ?

  • Survey research indicates that graduates of Catholic high schools are more likely to complete college and, on average, they earn more money than students who dont attend a Catholic high school.

  • A higher percentage of Catholics who attend Catholic high schools report that they continue to practice the faith.

  • Superior education positions your child for a lifetime of opportunity. Last year our graduating senior class received a total of $3.5 million in college scholarships.

  • Caring and supportive learning environments enhance your childs self-esteem. The value of having your child supported by the dynamic community that exists at Regina High School is beyond measure.

  • The single-gender environment provides a unique opportunity for young women to gain confidence in themselves and their abilities. They are free to pursue academic challenges and roles of leadership without the additional social pressures of a mixed-gender classroom.

You understand these values, but you may need a plan to finance your childs ability to experience them at Regina High School . Here is a review of the tuition at Regina High School as well as the financial options available to you.

Here are some options available to pay your tuition.

Option # 1: Pay the entire amount of the Tuition and Comprehensive Fee in advance before May 31, 2010 and receive a tuition discount of $250.

Option # 2: Sign-up for Automated Deductions from your checking or savings account.There are three payment plans available with Automated Deduction.

Plan A: 2 Payments. A tuition discount of $175 is provided if you

choose this option. (4/5/2010 & 1/5/2011).

Plan B: 12 Payments. A tuition discount of $100 is provided if

you choose this option. (4/5/2010 thru 3/5/2011)

Plan C: 24 Payments. A tuition discount of $50 is provided if you

choose this option. (5th and 20th of every month, 4/5/2010 thru 3/20/2011).

Option # 3: Use the payment coupon book supplied and make 12 monthly payments via check, money order, or cash by the 5th of every month, either by mail or in person at the school. Credit card payments (Visa, Master Card and Discover only) are also accepted via phone or in person in the Business Office. There is a 2.5% convenience charge added for all credit card transactions.

Unless you clearly feel that you will not be eligible, you should apply for institutional financial assistance at Regina High School . On our website you can find a link to the Private School Aid Service (PSAS) application, which requires 2009 tax information. All information is kept strictly confidential. Forms are mailed to PSAS, which sends a report to Regina High School. If you decide to apply and need help completing the application, please call Regina s main office.

Regina High School wants to be a support to you in this process of financing tuition. We want to make sure that you have investigated and understand every option available to you. If you have any questions, please contact us at 586-585-0500.

Sincerely,

Sr. Mary Leanne

P.S. Material things we purchase every day like automobiles come and go, but your daughters education will last a lifetime.
