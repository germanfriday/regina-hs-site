LOOKING TO REDUCE THE COST OF YOUR TUITION?

If so, and you are going to purchase groceries in the next week, fill up your car with gas, or purchase gift cards to give as gifts, then SCRIP is a great way for you to lower your tuition costs!

SCRIP is a brilliant fund raising program that allows you to earn tuition credit without costing you one penny more that you are already spending!!

SCRIP IS “REAL” MONEY – When you purchase SCRIP, you are purchasing actual gift cards that are used just like cash.

SCRIP PAYS – Gift cards are purchased at a discounted rate by De La Salle SCRIP (for Regina families). 75% of that discount (varies per vendor) is later realized as your “profit” when sold to you at face value. That’s it! A dollar-per-dollar exchange!

FAMILIES LOVE THE TUITION SAVINGS – Participating families regularly earn hundreds of dollars off of their tuition each year with very little effort. Some families earn $1,000 or more in one year! It’s just a matter of changing your thought pattern. Make your money work for you!

THERE ARE SO MANY WAYS TO SAVE – Groceries: Kroger, Nino’s, Sam’s, Randazzo’s, and Vince & Joe’s. Department Stores: JC Penney, Kohl’s, Kmart and Wal-Mart. Gas: Sunoco, Mobil, Speedway, and BP. Restaurants Galore RX: CVS, Walgreen’s, and Rite Aid. Specialty Stores, Entertainment, and Services. There are hundreds of vendors to choose from!

*** Please Note: You MUST enroll with SCRIP to participate in Kroger Community Rewards. Please complete, sign and turn in the SCRIP Enrollment/Release Form. You may fax, mail, email as an attachment or send into the school office. ***
