Alion Science and Technology Grant

Regina High School was presented with the 2010 Alion Science and Technology Grant to help further the school’s science, technology, and math programs.  Charles Ware, Vice President/Operation Manager of Training and Analysis Operation presented the award to Sister Mary Leanne, the school’s Principal. This award is part of Alion’s continuing effort to promote technology in secondary schools in communities throughout the nation.  Sister Leanne plans to use the grant to further the Saddlebots, Regina High School ’s robotics team. Also present from Alion were Joel King, R&D Support Division Manager and pictured with Sr. Leanne and Charles Ware is Tom Judd, the Robotics Moderator and “Francis” our Saddlebot.
